Instructions

Let's break it down!

When you start the game, you'll have five minutes to rack up the most points as you can. You know, pretty standard.

To earn points, you have to find words that match the criteria given to you.

The 1st criterion is simple and obvious: the first letter of the word.

The 2nd is where your brain power and speed come into play. In the input box, you must quickly find a word whose letters sum up to a target value, plus or minus a given amount. When you're ready, hit enter and your word will be verified with a dictionary.

For instance, if the target is 50, you need to find a word that consists of letters that add up (a=1, b=2, c=3 and so on) to 50, give or take five. In other words, any value between 45 and 55 will be accepted and you'll move on to the next word. Note that at higher targets, you'll be given more leeway.

If you hit a target exactly, you get 20 points. However, for every value away from the target, you get dinged 1 point.

As an example, 'hello' comes to 52. With a target of 50, you'd make 18 points because you were two off.

Likewise, if you'd like to 'Pass' on a set of criteria, you may but this incurs a loss of 10 points.

Good luck! Click "New Game" to begin.
